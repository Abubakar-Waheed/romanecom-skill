```text
Use $romanecom to screen this ecommerce acquisition:

Revenue: $32M
EBITDA: $4.1M
Subscription revenue: 78%
Category trend: pet longevity and at-home care rising
Whale: Chewy and large pet-health platforms educating the market
Hero SKU: monthly arthritis and joint-care subscription
Gross margin: 58%
Delivered-order variable costs: 21% of revenue
Marketing: 14% of revenue
OPEX: 10% of revenue
Expensive FTEs: 42
Supplier terms: net 75
Production + freight + inbound: 28 days

```

Expected shape:

- Decide whether this is a category-quality asset or only a durable old brand.
- Show the PC ladder.
- Identify where OPEX and PC2 can be repaired.
- Estimate whether payment terms support distributions.
- Name deal-breakers before recommending a bid.
